## Key Discussion Points

### The Concept
Wander is an AI-powered urban walking platform that converts pedestrian movement into verified, performance-based local commerce. Full thesis lives in [/concept/wander-manifesto.md](../../concept/wander-manifesto.md).

### GTM Hypothesis (Cruise Corridor)
- **Target market:** Seattle, Port of Seattle cruise disembarkation
- **User profile:** Cruise passengers with 4-6 hours of shore time, no car, unfamiliar with the city, in "spend on experiences" mode
- **Acquisition thesis:** QR code at disembarkation point, positioned as a value-added excursion alternative; rev share with cruise operator
- **Data-first sequencing:** Months 1-2 focus on foot traffic capture, not revenue; use that data to pitch merchants ("15,000 people walked past your door")

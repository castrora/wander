Log a new decision to the Wander decision log.

Gather the following (from conversation context or by asking):
- What was decided
- Why (the rationale)
- What alternatives were considered and rejected
- Who owns the decision

Then append a new entry to pm/decisions/decision-log.md using the next D-### number and today's date.

Format:
---
## D-### — [Short Title]
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD
**Status:** Active
**Decision:** [What was decided, one sentence]
**Rationale:** [Why]
**Alternatives Considered:** [What was rejected and why]
**Owner:** [Ray Castro / Claude / Joint]
---

Confirm to the user that the decision has been logged and give the decision ID.

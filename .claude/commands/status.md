Generate a weekly status report for the Wander project.

Read the following files before generating the report:
- pm/decisions/decision-log.md
- pm/risks/risk-log.md
- pm/meetings/ (most recent meeting file)
- pm/status/ (most recent status file, for comparison)

Then produce a status report in this format and save it to pm/status/YYYY-MM-DD-status.md (use today's date):

---
# Weekly Status Report — [Date]

## Overall Status: [GREEN / YELLOW / RED]
*Green = on track. Yellow = at risk but recoverable. Red = blocked or off track.*

## Progress Since Last Report
- [What was completed or moved forward]

## This Week's Focus
- [What needs to happen in the next 7 days]

## Open Risks (Top 3)
- [Risk, likelihood, mitigation status]

## Open Actions (Overdue or Due This Week)
- [Action, owner, due date, status]

## Decisions Needed
- [Any decision that is blocking progress]

## Notes / Observations
- [Anything the PM wants Ray to know]
---

After saving the file, summarize the key points in the conversation.

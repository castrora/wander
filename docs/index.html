How It Works

From ship to street
in 30 seconds.

Wander removes the friction between disembarkation and discovery. No planning required. No app store required. Just scan and go.

1

Scan the QR Code

Passengers scan a Wander QR code at the terminal, hotel lobby, or concierge desk. No app download required — opens instantly in any browser.

2

Set Your Parameters

Tell Wander how much time you have and what you're interested in — history, food, waterfront, architecture. Wander builds a personalized loop in seconds.

3

Walk with a Story

AI narration brings the city to life as you walk. Contextual merchant suggestions appear naturally in the story — optional, never interruptive.

4

Verified Commerce

When a walker enters a merchant's location, Wander verifies the visit and charges the merchant a small CPV fee. No impressions. No clicks. Just real foot traffic.
